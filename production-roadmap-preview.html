Roadmap

The road to production

From Founder allocation to first delivery.

After Founder 100 selections are secured, KAIZURO moves from allocation into production planning, manufacturing, Sydney handover and Founder delivery.

01

Founder allocation secured

Founder position, model choice and accessory preferences are registered.

02

Founder 100 closes

The allocation closes when all 100 Founder positions are secured or on the close date, whichever comes first.

03

Production priorities confirmed

KAIZURO reviews Founder model, colour and accessory selections to guide the first production run.

04

Manufacturing commences

Founder rods move into controlled manufacturing based on fixed engineering specifications.

05

Quality assurance & packing in Sydney

Each Founder rod passes through KAIZURO's Sydney handover process, including pre-dispatch inspection, registration and Founder pack preparation.

06

Founder deliveries begin

Founder rods, accessories and registration details are packed and dispatched from Sydney to each Founder.
