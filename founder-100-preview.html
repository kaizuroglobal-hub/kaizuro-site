Founder 100 Program

The first 100 shape what comes next.

The KAIZURO Founder 100 Program is a limited first allocation for the first 100 KAIZURO owners.

Rather than launching every model, finish and accessory combination into full production immediately, KAIZURO will use Founder 100 selections to confirm which platforms, colours and accessories should move first into the final production range.

The Founder 100 Program will directly feed into the final production range.

Founder choices across model, colour and accessory selection will help determine KAIZURO's first production priorities. Once the Founder 100 allocation closes, KAIZURO will move into production planning based on the strongest Founder selections.
